# Industry Benchmark Metrics — Priming RAG

## Insights

Public industry benchmarks provide calibration points for discovery engagements. These metrics help set realistic expectations, identify gaps against industry standards, and justify transformation investments. All data sourced from publicly available research reports and industry standards bodies.

## Key Points

- DORA metrics are the industry standard for software delivery performance measurement
- Digital maturity assessments correlate strongly with revenue growth and operational efficiency
- Test automation ROI typically reaches break-even within 6-12 months for repetitive test suites
- Cloud migration programs average 20-30% infrastructure cost reduction after optimization
- AI/ML projects have a 50-85% failure rate in production deployment (Gartner, 2024)
