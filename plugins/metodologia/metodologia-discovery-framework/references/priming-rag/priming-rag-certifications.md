# Public Certification Frameworks — Priming RAG

## Insights

Certification frameworks provide standardized maturity models and competency baselines relevant to discovery engagements. During AS-IS analysis and scenario evaluation, referencing applicable certifications helps calibrate current state, identify gaps, and recommend improvement paths. All frameworks listed are publicly available standards.

## Key Points

- Certifications serve as objective maturity indicators during discovery
- Cloud certifications (AWS, Azure, GCP) map directly to team capability assessment
- ISTQB provides the industry standard for QA competency levels
- PMI/PMP and SAFe certifications indicate project management maturity
- ISO standards provide compliance baselines for security, quality, and data governance
