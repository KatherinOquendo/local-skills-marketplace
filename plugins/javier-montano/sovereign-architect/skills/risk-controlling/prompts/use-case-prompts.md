# Risk Controlling — Use Case Prompts

## Prompt 1 — Cloud Migration Risk Register

> Produce a comprehensive risk register for migrating 30 applications to AWS.
> Cover technical risks (data loss, performance regression), organizational risks
> (skill gaps, change resistance), and commercial risks (cost overrun, vendor lock-in).
> Include mitigation strategies, owners, and early warning indicators.

## Prompt 2 — Third-Party Dependency Risk Assessment

> Assess the risks of depending on a single vendor for the payment processing
> infrastructure. Analyze vendor lock-in, SLA exposure, compliance implications,
> and business continuity. Produce a risk heat map and contingency plan for
> vendor failure scenarios.

## Prompt 3 — Greenfield Architecture Risk Analysis

> Identify and assess risks for a greenfield event-driven microservices architecture.
> Cover technology maturity, team capability gaps, operational complexity, and
> integration risks. Use pre-mortem analysis to surface non-obvious failure modes.
> Produce the risk register with cascading failure scenarios.
