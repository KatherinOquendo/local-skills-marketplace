# Stakeholder Mapping — Body of Knowledge

## Core Concepts
- **Influence/Interest Matrix**: 2x2 grid classifying stakeholders by their power to affect outcomes and their concern with outcomes
- **RACI Matrix**: Responsibility assignment: Responsible (does the work), Accountable (owns the outcome), Consulted (provides input), Informed (notified of outcomes)
- **Stakeholder Engagement**: Strategies for managing stakeholder expectations, concerns, and involvement
- **Decision-Making Authority**: Understanding who can approve, veto, or influence technical decisions
- **Communication Cadence**: Structured schedule of updates tailored to stakeholder needs and influence level
- **Change Management**: Managing stakeholder reactions to technical changes and new system adoption

## Patterns
- **Architecture Review Board**: Cross-functional committee for significant architectural decisions
- **Decision Log**: Transparent record of decisions made, rationale, and stakeholders involved
- **Technical Advisory Committee**: Expert group providing guidance without decision authority
- **Town Hall + Office Hours**: Combination of broadcast updates and individual Q&A sessions
- **Architecture Brief**: Concise, non-technical summary of architectural decisions for business stakeholders

## Tools & Frameworks
- **Miro / Mural**: Visual collaboration for stakeholder mapping workshops
- **Confluence / Notion**: Documentation for RACI matrices and communication plans
- **Jira / Linear**: Decision tracking and action item management
- **Slack / Teams**: Asynchronous communication channels per stakeholder group
- **Loom / Screen recordings**: Asynchronous technical briefings for distributed stakeholders
