# Lessons Learned Tracker — Use Case Prompts

## Prompt 1: Capture a Correction
> I just corrected your output — you used Sofka brand colors (green) in a JM Labs deliverable. Capture this as a lesson learned. Record: what went wrong, why, the correct behavior, a one-line rule, and the preventive measure to apply. Classify severity and category.

## Prompt 2: Pre-Flight Lesson Review
> I am about to generate a technical proposal for a banking client. Review the lessons-learned registry and produce a pre-flight checklist of all applicable lessons. Group them by category (formatting, technical accuracy, branding, process) and present as a checkable list I can review before starting.

## Prompt 3: Monthly Lessons Review
> Conduct a monthly review of the lessons-learned registry. Produce: total lessons captured this month, breakdown by category and severity, regression rate (how many recurring mistakes), top 3 systemic issues identified from lesson clusters, and recommended process changes to address them. Archive any lessons that have been fully resolved with no recurrence in 30 days.
