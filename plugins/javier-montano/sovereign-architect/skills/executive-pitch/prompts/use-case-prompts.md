# Executive Pitch — Use Case Prompts

## Prompt 1 — Platform Modernization Business Case

> Build an executive pitch for migrating a legacy monolith to a cloud-native
> microservices architecture. The audience is the CTO and CFO of a mid-market
> financial services company. Include 4 value pillars, 3-year ROI projection,
> and the cost of inaction if migration is delayed by 12 months.

## Prompt 2 — AI/ML Investment Justification

> Create a business case for establishing an internal ML platform team.
> Frame the investment against the alternative of per-project consulting
> engagements. Include time-to-value comparisons, capability building
> benefits, and a phased investment timeline with quarterly milestones.

## Prompt 3 — Developer Experience Initiative

> Pitch a developer experience (DevEx) initiative to the VP of Engineering.
> Quantify the impact of reducing build times, standardizing toolchains,
> and improving onboarding. Use DORA metrics as the value measurement
> framework and include before/after projections.
