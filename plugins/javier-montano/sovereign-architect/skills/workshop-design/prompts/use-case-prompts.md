# Workshop Design — Use Case Prompts

## Prompt 1 — Event Storming for Domain Discovery

> Design a full-day event storming workshop for discovering the bounded contexts
> of an e-commerce platform. Include pre-work for participants, the time-boxed
> agenda, facilitation guide with prompts for stuck moments, and output
> templates for capturing events, commands, aggregates, and policies.

## Prompt 2 — Architecture Decision Workshop

> Design a half-day architecture decision workshop for choosing between
> three database options. Participants: 2 architects, 3 senior engineers,
> 1 product owner. Design the evaluation framework, structured debate
> format, and decision capture template. Include the conflict resolution protocol.

## Prompt 3 — Impact Mapping Sprint

> Create a 3-hour impact mapping workshop for aligning a platform team's
> quarterly roadmap with business goals. Design the goal decomposition
> activity, actor identification, impact brainstorming, and deliverable
> mapping. Include the dot-voting prioritization step and the output
> template that becomes the team's quarterly plan.
