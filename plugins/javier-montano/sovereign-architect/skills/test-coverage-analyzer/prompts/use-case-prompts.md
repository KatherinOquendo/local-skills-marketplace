# Test Coverage Analyzer — Use Case Prompts

## Prompt 1: Coverage Gaps
> "Where are the critical coverage gaps? Focus on business logic, not boilerplate."

## Prompt 2: Test Quality Audit
> "Are our tests actually testing anything? Check for assertion quality, not just line coverage."

## Prompt 3: Testing Strategy
> "What types of tests are we missing? Do we have the right balance of unit, integration, and e2e?"
