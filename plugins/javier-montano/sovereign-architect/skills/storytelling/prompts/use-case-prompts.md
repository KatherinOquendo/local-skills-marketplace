# Storytelling — Use Case Prompts

## Prompt 1 — Architecture Assessment as Narrative

> Transform this architecture assessment (12 findings, 3 recommendations)
> into a narrative-driven presentation for the CTO. Design a three-act arc
> that builds from the current state through the analysis journey to the
> recommended transformation. Open with a hook based on the most impactful finding.

## Prompt 2 — Incident Post-Mortem as Story

> Reframe this post-mortem report as a narrative that builds understanding
> of how the outage unfolded. Use the timeline as the story's backbone,
> but structure it to reveal the root cause at the climax, not the opening.
> End with the resolution: what the team learned and what changes were made.

## Prompt 3 — Migration Success Story

> Craft a compelling narrative of the database migration from Oracle to PostgreSQL.
> The audience is the engineering organization (200 people). Use the hero's
> journey: the team faced a challenge, gained new capabilities, and emerged
> with measurable improvements. Include specific data points woven into
> the narrative, not as an appendix.
