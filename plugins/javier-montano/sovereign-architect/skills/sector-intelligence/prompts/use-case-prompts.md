# Sector Intelligence — Use Case Prompts

## Prompt 1 — Banking Sector Technology Brief

> Produce a sector intelligence brief for a regional bank evaluating
> cloud migration. Cover regulatory constraints (data sovereignty, SOX,
> PCI-DSS), competitive benchmarks (what do peer institutions use?),
> and emerging trends (open banking, real-time payments). Map constraints
> to specific architectural decisions.

## Prompt 2 — Healthcare Platform Compliance Context

> Provide industry context for a healthcare SaaS platform expansion.
> Map HIPAA, HITECH, and state-level privacy regulations to technical
> requirements. Benchmark against healthcare technology leaders in
> interoperability (FHIR adoption) and security posture. Identify
> upcoming regulatory changes that affect the 12-month roadmap.

## Prompt 3 — Retail Digital Transformation Landscape

> Analyze the retail sector technology landscape for a mid-size retailer
> considering headless commerce. Cover competitive technology stacks,
> peak traffic engineering patterns (seasonal scaling), and omnichannel
> integration trends. Benchmark DORA metrics against retail sector medians.
