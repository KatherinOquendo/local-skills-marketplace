# Risk Scorer — Use Case Prompts

## Prompt 1: Full Risk Assessment
> "Identify and score all technical risks in this codebase. Give me a risk matrix."

## Prompt 2: Critical Risks
> "What are the highest-severity risks in this project? What could go catastrophically wrong?"

## Prompt 3: Mitigation Plan
> "For our top 5 risks, create a mitigation plan with specific actions and priorities."
