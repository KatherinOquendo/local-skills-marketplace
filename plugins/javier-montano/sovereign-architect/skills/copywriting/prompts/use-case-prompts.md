# Copywriting — Use Case Prompts

## Prompt 1 — Platform Modernization Value Proposition

> Write the value proposition and executive summary for a platform modernization
> initiative targeting the CFO and CTO. Translate technical benefits (microservices,
> cloud-native, CI/CD) into business outcomes. Include a one-sentence pitch,
> a three-paragraph summary, and three calls to action for different decision stages.

## Prompt 2 — Developer Tool Internal Launch

> Craft the internal launch copy for a new developer experience platform.
> The audience is 200 engineers. Frame the value in terms they care about:
> reduced friction, faster feedback loops, less toil. Include a one-liner,
> a feature-benefit table, and a "get started in 5 minutes" CTA.

## Prompt 3 — Technical Assessment Executive Summary

> Transform a 30-page technical assessment into a 1-page executive summary
> for the board of directors. Preserve the three most critical findings,
> translate technical risk into business impact, and end with a clear
> investment recommendation and decision timeline.
