# Copywriting — Sample Output

## Context

An engineering team built a new observability platform and needs to persuade
other teams to adopt it. Current adoption: 2 of 15 teams. Target: 10 teams
in 90 days.

## Output

### One-Sentence Pitch

We help engineering teams find and fix production issues in minutes instead of hours
by unifying logs, metrics, and traces in a single searchable interface.

### Value Proposition (Three Paragraphs)

**The Problem**: Your team spends an average of 47 minutes per incident just locating
the relevant logs across three different tools. That is 47 minutes of customer impact,
47 minutes of context switching, and 47 minutes of stress that compounds every on-call
rotation. [DOC — incident metrics Q4 2025]

**The Change**: The Observability Platform unifies logs, metrics, and traces into a
single query interface with automated correlation. Teams using it have reduced their
mean-time-to-identify (MTTI) from 47 minutes to 8 minutes — an 83% improvement.
[CODE — platform metrics dashboard]

**The Path**: Onboarding takes less than one hour. Our team handles the initial
instrumentation setup for your top 3 services. You start seeing correlated data
in your first on-call shift.
