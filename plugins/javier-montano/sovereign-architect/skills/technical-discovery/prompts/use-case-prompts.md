# Technical Discovery — Use Case Prompts

## Prompt 1: New Team Onboarding Scan
> "I just joined this team and need to understand this codebase. Scan the repository and give me a complete technology manifest: languages, frameworks, databases, infrastructure, CI/CD, and entry points. Include version numbers where available."

## Prompt 2: Pre-Acquisition Technical Due Diligence
> "We're evaluating acquiring a company and need a technical inventory of their main product repository. Map the architecture, identify the tech stack, assess dependency health, and flag any structural concerns — but stick to facts only, no opinions."

## Prompt 3: Monorepo Package Mapping
> "This is a monorepo with multiple packages. Map out every package, its purpose, its internal dependencies on other packages, and its external dependencies. I need to understand the dependency graph before I can plan any changes."
