# Data Storytelling — Use Case Prompts

## Prompt 1 — DORA Metrics Improvement Story

> Transform 12 months of DORA metrics data into a compelling narrative for
> engineering leadership. The data shows deployment frequency increased 4x
> and lead time decreased 60%. Frame the data with before/after comparisons,
> industry benchmarks, and the key inflection point. Design visualizations
> that communicate the insight, not just the data.

## Prompt 2 — Cost Optimization Results

> Tell the data story of a cloud cost optimization initiative. Raw data:
> monthly spend reduced from $180K to $112K, but compute efficiency improved
> while handling 30% more traffic. Use magnitude communication to make
> the savings tangible and comparison framing to show this was not just
> cost cutting but efficiency improvement.

## Prompt 3 — Technical Debt Impact Quantification

> Create a data story that makes technical debt visible to non-technical
> stakeholders. Use metrics: 40% of sprint capacity goes to maintenance,
> average feature delivery time increased 3x over 2 years, incident
> frequency doubled. Frame the data to create urgency without alarm,
> and close with the investment case for debt reduction.
