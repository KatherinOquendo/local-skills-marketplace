# Cost Estimation — Use Case Prompts

## Prompt 1 — Project Effort Estimation
> "Estimate the effort for this software project. Decompose into work packages, identify effort inductors, apply sizing model, include non-functional overhead, and produce three-point estimates. Output a detailed breakdown with team composition and timeline ranges."

## Prompt 2 — Migration Cost Analysis
> "Estimate the effort for migrating this system to a new architecture/cloud. Identify scope drivers (services, databases, integrations), classify migration patterns per component, estimate effort per wave, and factor in risk multipliers. Produce a phased timeline with confidence ranges."

## Prompt 3 — Team Composition Design
> "Design the team composition for this engineering initiative. Identify required skills, calculate FTE needs per phase, factor in ramp-up time and context switching overhead, and produce a staffing plan with cost projections at 50%, 75%, and 90% confidence."
