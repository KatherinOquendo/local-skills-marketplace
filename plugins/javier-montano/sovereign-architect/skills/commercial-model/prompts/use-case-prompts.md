# Commercial Model — Use Case Prompts

## Prompt 1 — Identity Platform Build vs Buy

> Analyze the build-vs-buy decision for an identity and access management platform.
> Compare building on open-source (Keycloak), purchasing a COTS solution (Okta/Auth0),
> and building a custom solution. Include 5-year TCO, switching costs, and a weighted
> decision matrix. The organization has 50 engineers and 200K monthly active users.

## Prompt 2 — Data Platform Licensing Strategy

> Model the licensing costs and value capture for migrating from on-premise data
> warehouse (Teradata) to cloud-native alternatives (Snowflake, Databricks, BigQuery).
> Include compute, storage, egress, and engineering effort. Perform sensitivity
> analysis on data growth rate and query volume assumptions.

## Prompt 3 — Open Source Adoption TCO

> Calculate the true TCO of adopting an open-source orchestration platform (Temporal)
> vs a managed alternative (AWS Step Functions). Include self-hosting infrastructure,
> operational overhead, team training, and the cost of contributing upstream patches.
> Project over 3 years with team growth from 5 to 15 engineers.
