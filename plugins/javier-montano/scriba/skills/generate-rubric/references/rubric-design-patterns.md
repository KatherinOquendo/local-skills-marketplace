## Taxonomy-to-Rubric Mapping

### Bloom Levels and Rubric Verb Expectations

| Bloom Level | Descriptor Verbs (Basico) | Descriptor Verbs (Intermedio) | Descriptor Verbs (Avanzado) |
|-------------|--------------------------|------------------------------|----------------------------|
| Recordar | Reconoce parcialmente | Lista/nombra la mayoria | Lista todos y los contextualiza |
| Comprender | Explica con imprecisiones | Explica correctamente | Explica y conecta con otros conceptos |
| Aplicar | Aplica con errores | Aplica correctamente | Aplica en contextos nuevos |
| Analizar | Identifica algunas partes | Diferencia y organiza | Analiza relaciones complejas |
| Evaluar | Emite opinion sin justificacion | Justifica con argumentos | Evalua criticamente multiples perspectivas |
| Crear | Produce borrador incompleto | Produce trabajo coherente | Crea trabajo original e innovador |

## Performance Level Definitions

### 3-Level Model (Minimum)

| Level | Label | Score | Student-Facing Description |
|-------|-------|-------|---------------------------|
| Basico | En desarrollo | 1-2 | "Estoy aprendiendo. Necesito mas practica." |
| Intermedio | Logrado | 3-4 | "Lo logre. Puedo hacerlo con confianza." |
| Avanzado | Destacado | 5 | "Puedo ir mas alla. Domino este tema." |

### 4-Level Model (Extended)

| Level | Label | Score | Descriptor Pattern |
|-------|-------|-------|--------------------|
| Inicial | No alcanzado | 1 | Does not demonstrate the skill |
| Basico | En desarrollo | 2-3 | Demonstrates partially, with significant support |
| Intermedio | Logrado | 4-5 | Demonstrates independently with minor gaps |
| Avanzado | Destacado | 6-7 | Exceeds expectations, transfers to new contexts |

## Observable Descriptor Rules

### Do: Use Measurable Language

- "Identifica 3 de 4 elementos narrativos"
- "Resuelve 4 de 5 problemas sin error de calculo"
- "Produce un texto de al menos 8 oraciones con coherencia"
- "Clasifica al menos 6 de 8 items en las categorias correctas"

### Don't: Use Subjective Language

- ~~"Muestra buen conocimiento"~~ (what is "good"?)
- ~~"Comprende adecuadamente"~~ (not observable)
- ~~"Trabaja bien"~~ (no criteria)
- ~~"Demuestra interes"~~ (internal state, not observable)

## DUA-Compatible Assessment Patterns

### Principle 3: Multiple Means of Action & Expression

For every rubric criterion, offer at least 2 modality options:

| Criterion Type | Written Option | Oral Option | Visual Option |
|---------------|---------------|-------------|--------------|
| Identify elements | Written list | Verbal enumeration | Labeled diagram |
| Explain concept | Paragraph | Oral explanation (recorded) | Concept map with annotations |
| Compare/contrast | Essay | Debate/discussion | Venn diagram or table |
| Create product | Written story | Oral narration | Comic strip or video |
| Sequence events | Numbered list | Retelling | Timeline or flowchart |

### Rubric Footer Template

```markdown
**Modalidades aceptadas**: Escrita, oral, visual (diagrama, presentacion, maqueta) [DUA P3]
**Nota**: La calificacion se basa en el contenido demostrado, no en la modalidad elegida.
```

## Common Rubric Templates by Assessment Type

### Formative (During Unit)

- Fewer criteria (2-3), focus on process
- Descriptors emphasize effort and progress
- Used for feedback, not grading

### Summative (End of Unit)

- More criteria (4-6), focus on outcomes
- Descriptors emphasize mastery and transfer
- Used for grading and reporting
