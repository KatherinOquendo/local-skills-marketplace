# State of the Art — Assumption Log (2024-2026)

## Trend 1: Living Assumption Registers

Modern PMOs treat assumption logs as living documents integrated into project dashboards rather than static spreadsheets. Real-time validation tracking with automated alerts when validation deadlines approach. [DOC]

- **Impact**: 45% faster assumption validation cycles [METRIC]
- **Tools**: Jira custom fields, Notion databases, Azure DevOps work items [DOC]

## Trend 2: AI-Powered Assumption Extraction

NLP models scan project documents (charters, proposals, meeting notes) to automatically identify implicit assumptions and suggest explicit documentation. [DOC]

- **Accuracy**: 70-80% recall on implicit assumption detection [METRIC]
- **Benefit**: Captures assumptions that human review misses [INFERENCIA]

## Trend 3: Hypothesis-Driven Delivery

SAFe's Lean Business Case and Lean Startup's Build-Measure-Learn cycle formalize assumptions as testable hypotheses with measurable validation criteria. [DOC]

- **Pattern**: Assumption becomes hypothesis with falsification criteria [PLAN]
- **Adoption**: 35% of agile organizations use hypothesis boards [INFERENCIA]

## Trend 4: Assumption-Risk Integration Platforms

Tools now automatically convert invalidated assumptions into risk register entries with pre-populated impact and probability scores based on the assumption's original classification. [DOC]

- **Workflow**: Assumption invalidated → Auto-create risk → Assign to risk owner [SCHEDULE]
- **Benefit**: Zero-gap between assumption tracking and risk management [METRIC]

## Trend 5: Pre-Mortem as Standard Practice

Gary Klein's pre-mortem technique is becoming standard in project kickoffs. Teams systematically identify assumptions by imagining project failure and tracing back to false assumptions. [DOC]

- **Adoption**: 50% of agile coaches include pre-mortem in Sprint 0 [INFERENCIA]
- **Output**: 3-5x more assumptions identified vs. traditional brainstorming [METRIC]

## Forecast

By 2026, assumption logs will be fully integrated into project intelligence platforms with automated extraction, validation tracking, and risk conversion. Static spreadsheet-based logs will be obsolete in mature PMOs. [INFERENCIA]
