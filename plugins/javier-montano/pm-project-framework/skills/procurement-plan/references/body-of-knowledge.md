# Procurement Planning — Body of Knowledge

## Standards & Frameworks
Core knowledge areas: Make-or-buy analysis, contract types (FFP, T&M, CPFF, CPIF), SOW/SOO development, vendor evaluation and selection, RFx process management, procurement risk, source selection methods.

## PMBOK Alignment
- Integrates with PMBOK 7th Edition performance domains (Planning, Stakeholder, Delivery)
- Supports ISO 21502:2020 project governance requirements
- Aligns with PMBOK Procurement Management knowledge area (Plan, Conduct, Control, Close)
- Compatible with PRINCE2, SAFe, and hybrid methodologies

## Key Concepts

### Inputs
- Project scope statement and WBS (identifies what needs to be procured)
- Make-or-buy analysis results
- Budget baseline with procurement allocation
- Risk register (vendor-related risks)
- Organizational procurement policies and templates
- Market conditions and vendor landscape analysis
- Historical vendor performance data

### Contract Types
1. **Fixed-Price (FFP/FPIF)**: Known scope, low buyer risk, requires detailed SOW
2. **Time & Materials (T&M)**: Uncertain scope, shared risk, requires rate ceilings
3. **Cost-Reimbursable (CPFF/CPIF/CPAF)**: High uncertainty, buyer bears cost risk
4. **Outcome-Based**: Payment tied to business outcomes, shared risk/reward
5. **Hybrid**: Combined elements for complex procurements

### Process Steps
1. Identify procurement needs from WBS and make-or-buy analysis
2. Develop procurement strategy (contract types, sourcing approach)
3. Prepare SOW/SOO for each procurement package
4. Define evaluation criteria with weightings
5. Develop RFx documents (RFI, RFP, RFQ as appropriate)
6. Conduct market research and vendor shortlisting
7. Issue RFx and manage Q&A period
8. Evaluate proposals against weighted criteria
9. Negotiate and award contracts
10. Establish vendor management and performance monitoring framework

### Outputs
- Procurement management plan
- SOW/SOO documents per procurement package
- RFx documents with evaluation criteria
- Vendor evaluation scorecards
- Contract award recommendations with justification
- Vendor management plan with KPIs

## Quality Criteria
| Criterion | Threshold | Evidence Tag |
|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| Make-or-buy justification | Documented for each package | [DECISION] Required |
| SOW completeness | Acceptance criteria defined | [PLAN] Required |
| Evaluation criteria weights | Sum to 100%, approved by sponsor | [METRIC] Required |
| Vendor due diligence | Financial + technical + reference checks | [STAKEHOLDER] Required |
| Contract type justification | Risk allocation documented | [DECISION] Recommended |
| Historical vendor data | >=1 reference per vendor | [HISTORICO] Recommended |

## Integration Points
- Feeds into: project schedule (lead times), cost baseline (vendor costs), risk register (vendor risks)
- Receives from: scope statement, WBS, budget baseline, organizational procurement policies
- Cross-references: risk management, stakeholder management, schedule management, cost management
