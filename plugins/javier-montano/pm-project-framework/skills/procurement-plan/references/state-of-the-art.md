# Procurement Planning — State of the Art (2024–2026)

## Trend 1: AI-Assisted Vendor Evaluation
Machine learning models score vendor proposals against weighted criteria, detect anomalies in pricing, and predict vendor performance based on historical data. NLP analyzes proposal quality and compliance with SOW requirements.

## Trend 2: Outcome-Based Contracting
Shifting from deliverable-based to value-based procurement models. Contracts increasingly tie payments to measurable business outcomes rather than task completion, aligning vendor incentives with project success.

## Trend 3: Digital Procurement Platforms
End-to-end procurement platforms automate RFx creation, vendor communication, proposal evaluation, and contract management. Integration with PM tools provides real-time procurement status visibility within project dashboards.

## Trend 4: Supply Chain Risk Intelligence
Real-time monitoring of vendor financial health, geopolitical risks, and supply chain disruptions. Predictive models alert PMOs to potential vendor failures before they impact project delivery.

## Trend 5: Sustainable and Ethical Procurement
ESG criteria increasingly incorporated into vendor evaluation. Organizations require carbon footprint reporting, labor practice certifications, and diversity metrics from vendors, adding new dimensions to the selection process.
