# State of the Art — Funding Request (2024-2026)

## Trend 1: AI-Augmented Funding Proposals

Organizations are leveraging generative AI to draft funding requests, auto-populate financial models, and generate scenario analyses. AI tools reduce preparation time by 40-60% while improving consistency across portfolio submissions. [DOC]

- **Evidence**: Gartner 2025 PPM survey reports 35% of enterprises use AI in budget justification workflows
- **Impact**: Democratizes high-quality proposals; risk of over-reliance on generated narratives
- **Maturity**: Early Majority adoption phase

## Trend 2: Continuous Funding Models

The shift from annual budgeting to continuous funding mirrors agile delivery patterns. Organizations adopt quarterly investment reviews with lean business cases replacing heavyweight proposals. [INFERENCIA]

- **Evidence**: SAFe 6.0 and Disciplined Agile promote lean budgets at value-stream level
- **Impact**: Faster time-to-funding; requires stronger portfolio governance
- **Maturity**: Early Adopter to Early Majority

## Trend 3: Outcome-Based Funding

Funding tied to measurable outcomes (OKRs, KPIs) rather than activity completion. Disbursements linked to value delivered, not milestones passed. [DOC]

- **Evidence**: McKinsey 2024 report on "Value-Based Resource Allocation"
- **Impact**: Better ROI tracking; challenges in defining leading indicators
- **Maturity**: Innovator to Early Adopter

## Trend 4: ESG-Integrated Funding Criteria

Environmental, Social, and Governance factors increasingly required in funding justifications. Projects must demonstrate sustainability impact alongside financial returns. [DOC]

- **Evidence**: EU Corporate Sustainability Reporting Directive (CSRD) 2024 requirements
- **Impact**: Additional compliance burden; competitive advantage for ESG-positive projects
- **Maturity**: Regulatory-driven adoption

## Trend 5: Decentralized Decision-Making

Empowered teams allocate budgets within guardrails, reducing central approval bottlenecks. Decision authority pushed to value-stream level with portfolio-level oversight. [INFERENCIA]

- **Evidence**: Beyond Budgeting Institute case studies (2024-2025)
- **Impact**: Faster decisions; requires trust and transparency infrastructure
- **Maturity**: Early Adopter
