# State of the Art — SAFe Assessment (2024-2026)

## Trend 1: Continuous Assessment over Periodic Audits

Organizations shifting from annual SAFe assessments to continuous maturity tracking using automated metrics from DevOps toolchains and project management platforms. [DOC]

> "Principio Rector: La agilidad se mide en resultados, no en ceremonias."

## Trend 2: Outcome-Based SAFe Metrics

Moving beyond process compliance metrics (do we do PI Planning?) to outcome metrics (are we delivering value faster?). DORA metrics integrated as primary SAFe health indicators. [INFERENCIA]

## Trend 3: AI-Assisted Maturity Scoring

AI tools analyze organizational data (Jira, Git, CI/CD, Slack) to automatically generate SAFe maturity scores and identify improvement opportunities without manual assessment effort. [DOC]

## Trend 4: SAFe Lean Governance Assessment

Focus expanding beyond team and ART maturity to include Lean Portfolio Management assessment — how well organizations manage strategy, funding, and governance at the portfolio level. [INFERENCIA]

## Trend 5: Cultural Assessment Integration

Technical SAFe maturity assessments now include cultural dimensions — psychological safety, learning culture, experimentation willingness — recognizing that mindset transformation is as important as process adoption. [DOC]

| Assessment Domain | Traditional Focus | Modern Focus |
|------------------|------------------|-------------|
| Team level | Ceremony compliance | Value delivery speed |
| ART level | PI Planning execution | Feature cycle time |
| Portfolio | Budget allocation | Strategic agility |
| Culture | Training completion | Psychological safety |
