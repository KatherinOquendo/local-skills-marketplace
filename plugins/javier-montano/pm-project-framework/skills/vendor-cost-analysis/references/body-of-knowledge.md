# Body of Knowledge — Vendor Cost Analysis

## 1. Definition and Scope

Vendor cost analysis evaluates the total cost of engaging external vendors, including direct costs (rates, licenses), indirect costs (management overhead, integration), and hidden costs (knowledge transfer, ramp-up, risk). It supports make-vs-buy decisions and vendor selection. [DOC]

## 2. Foundational Frameworks

| Framework | Source | Key Contribution |
|-----------|--------|-----------------|
| Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) | Gartner | Full lifecycle cost model |
| PMBOK Procurement Management | PMI, 2021 | Vendor evaluation and selection |
| Should-Cost Analysis | Supply Chain | Bottom-up cost estimation |
| Activity-Based Costing | Cooper & Kaplan | Overhead allocation to activities |
| Vendor Rating Models | ISM/CIPS | Multi-criteria vendor scoring |

## 3. Cost Categories

| Category | Examples | Visibility |
|----------|---------|:----------:|
| Direct costs | Hourly rates, fixed fees, licenses | High [METRIC] |
| Transition costs | Onboarding, knowledge transfer, tooling setup | Medium |
| Management costs | Vendor management, status meetings, coordination | Low |
| Quality costs | Rework, additional testing, defect resolution | Low |
| Risk costs | Delays, vendor failure contingency, IP protection | Very low |
| Exit costs | Transition out, knowledge extraction, contract wind-down | Very low |

## 4. TCO Model

| Phase | Cost Elements | Duration |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| Selection | RFP process, evaluation, negotiation | 4-8 weeks |
| Onboarding | Knowledge transfer, access setup, ramp-up | 2-6 weeks |
| Delivery | Service delivery, management, quality assurance | Project duration |
| Transition out | Knowledge extraction, handover, contract closure | 2-4 weeks |

## 5. Make vs. Buy Decision Framework

| Factor | Build Internal | Buy External |
|--------|:-------------:|:------------:|
| Strategic capability | Build | Buy |
| Temporary need | Buy | Buy |
| Core competency | Build | Avoid |
| Time pressure | Buy (short-term) | Buy |
| Cost (long-term) | Often lower | Often higher |
| Control | High | Lower |
| Flexibility | Lower | Higher |

## 6. Key Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Cost variance | Actual cost / Estimated cost | 0.95-1.05 |
| Cost per deliverable | Total vendor cost / Deliverables completed | Declining trend |
| Management overhead ratio | Management cost / Total vendor cost | < 15% |
| Quality cost ratio | Rework cost / Total vendor cost | < 10% [METRIC] |
| TCO vs. internal benchmark | Vendor TCO / Internal delivery cost | < 1.0 for outsourcing |
