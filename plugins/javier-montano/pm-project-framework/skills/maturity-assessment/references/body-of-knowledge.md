## 1. Foundational Frameworks

### OPM3 (Organizational Project Management Maturity Model)
- **Source**: PMI *OPM3* 3rd Ed. (2013) [DOC]
- **Scope**: Project, program, and portfolio management maturity across standardize, measure, control, and continuously improve [DOC]
- **Application**: Comprehensive assessment for PMI-aligned organizations [DOC]

### P3M3 (Portfolio, Programme and Project Management Maturity Model)
- **Source**: Axelos *P3M3* (2015) [DOC]
- **Levels**: 1-Awareness, 2-Repeatable, 3-Defined, 4-Managed, 5-Optimized [DOC]
- **Perspectives**: 7 perspectives per domain (management control, benefits, financial, stakeholder, risk, organizational governance, resource) [DOC]

### CMMI (Capability Maturity Model Integration)
- **Source**: ISACA *CMMI V2.0* (2018) [DOC]
- **Application**: Process maturity assessment adaptable to PM context [DOC]

## 3. Maturity Level Taxonomy

### Generic Maturity Levels (Cross-Framework)
| Level | Name | Characteristics | Evidence |
|:---:|------|----------------|----------|
| 1 | Initial/Ad hoc | No standardized processes; hero-dependent delivery | Inconsistent project outcomes [METRIC] |
| 2 | Repeatable | Basic processes for similar projects; reactive management | Some templates exist [PLAN] |
| 3 | Defined | Standardized processes; proactive management | Process documentation complete [DOC] |
| 4 | Managed | Processes measured and controlled; data-driven decisions | Metrics dashboards active [METRIC] |
| 5 | Optimizing | Continuous improvement embedded; innovation culture | Improvement experiments tracked [METRIC] |

### Assessment Domain Taxonomy
| Domain | Sub-domains | Weight |
|--------|------------|:---:|
| Project Management | Scope, schedule, cost, quality, risk, resources | 35% [PLAN] |
| Program Management | Benefits, stakeholder, governance, integration | 25% [PLAN] |
| Portfolio Management | Strategic alignment, prioritization, optimization | 20% [PLAN] |
| Organizational Enablers | People, process, technology, governance structure | 20% [PLAN] |

## 5. Scoring Methodology

### Evidence-Based Scoring Protocol
1. **Artifact review**: Process documents, templates, tools in use [DOC]
2. **Performance data**: On-time, on-budget, on-scope rates [METRIC]
3. **Practitioner interviews**: PM, sponsor, team perspectives [STAKEHOLDER]
4. **Tool utilization**: Actual usage vs. licensed capacity [METRIC]
5. **Governance compliance**: Adherence to established processes [PLAN]

### Calibration Rules
- Minimum 2 evidence sources per domain score [PLAN]
- Self-reported scores validated against artifacts [PLAN]
- Department scores not averaged — extremes preserved [METRIC]
