# Metaprompts — Kickoff Package

## Metaprompt 1: Kickoff Completeness Checker

**Strategy**: Verify the kickoff package covers all essential dimensions for project success.

**Protocol**:
1. Check against a comprehensive kickoff checklist (12 categories)
2. For each missing element, assess risk of proceeding without it
3. Differentiate "must have before kickoff" from "can develop during Sprint 0"
4. Ensure sponsor presence and charter authorization are confirmed
5. Rate overall readiness on a 1-5 scale with go/no-go recommendation

**Activation**:
> "Audit this kickoff package for completeness. Check every essential element against best practice. For each gap, assess the risk of proceeding without it. Give a readiness score and go/no-go recommendation."

## Metaprompt 2: Stakeholder Engagement Optimizer

**Strategy**: Design the kickoff to maximize engagement for different stakeholder types.

**Protocol**:
1. Classify stakeholders by interest and influence
2. Identify what each stakeholder group needs from the kickoff
3. Design agenda segments that address each group's needs
4. Include interaction points to maintain engagement
5. Plan follow-up tailored to each group's role

**Activation**:
> "Optimize this kickoff for stakeholder engagement. What does each stakeholder group need to hear and contribute? Design the agenda to maximize relevance for each group. Plan differentiated follow-up based on stakeholder role."

## Metaprompt 3: Team Dynamics Primer

**Strategy**: Use the kickoff to accelerate team forming by addressing potential friction points proactively.

**Protocol**:
1. Identify team composition (roles, experience, cultural backgrounds)
2. Anticipate potential friction points (methodology preferences, communication styles)
3. Design team-building exercises that surface and address these proactively
4. Include working agreement creation as a kickoff activity
5. Plan ongoing team health monitoring post-kickoff

**Activation**:
> "Design kickoff team-building activities that proactively address likely friction points. Consider the team composition and anticipate where misalignment might occur. Include working agreement creation and team health monitoring setup."
