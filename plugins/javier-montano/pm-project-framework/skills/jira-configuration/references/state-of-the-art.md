# State of the Art — Jira Configuration (2024-2026)

## Trend 1: AI-Powered Jira Automation

Atlassian Intelligence integrates AI into Jira for smart issue summarization, work suggestion, and natural language JQL queries. Configuration becomes conversational. [DOC]

- **Evidence**: Atlassian Intelligence GA release 2024; AI features in Jira Cloud Premium
- **Impact**: Lower barrier to complex queries; reduced manual triage
- **Maturity**: Early Majority

## Trend 2: Jira Align for Enterprise Portfolio

Jira Align connects team-level Jira data to portfolio-level strategic planning, enabling SAFe, LeSS, and enterprise Kanban at scale. [DOC]

- **Evidence**: Atlassian Jira Align adoption by 500+ enterprises (2025)
- **Impact**: End-to-end traceability from strategy to sprint; complex setup
- **Maturity**: Early Majority

## Trend 3: Low-Code Workflow Automation

Jira Automation and third-party tools (ScriptRunner, Adaptavist) enable complex workflow automation without coding, making PM process enforcement easier. [DOC]

- **Evidence**: Jira Automation processes 1B+ rules per month across cloud instances
- **Impact**: Process consistency; risk of automation sprawl
- **Maturity**: Late Majority

## Trend 4: Integrated DevOps Metrics

Jira connects to CI/CD pipelines (GitHub, Bitbucket, Jenkins) to surface DORA metrics alongside PM metrics in unified dashboards. [INFERENCIA]

- **Evidence**: Jira DevOps integrations and Compass platform launch (2024)
- **Impact**: Holistic delivery visibility; requires DevOps toolchain integration
- **Maturity**: Early Majority
