# Body of Knowledge — Risk Monitoring

## 1. Definition and Scope

Risk monitoring is the continuous process of tracking identified risks, detecting new risks, evaluating risk response effectiveness, and reporting risk status to stakeholders. It transforms static risk registers into living early-warning systems. [DOC]

## 3. Monitoring Activities

| Activity | Frequency | Output |
|----------|-----------|--------|
| Risk register review | Sprint/Weekly | Updated risk register [PLAN] |
| Risk metric tracking | Continuous | Dashboard updates [METRIC] |
| Trigger condition monitoring | Continuous | Alert notifications |
| Risk response audit | Monthly | Effectiveness assessment |
| Emerging risk identification | Sprint/Weekly | New risk entries |
| Risk escalation review | As triggered | Escalation decisions |

## 4. Risk Indicators

| Indicator Type | Description | Example |
|---------------|-------------|---------|
| Key Risk Indicator (KRI) | Leading metric predicting risk materialization | Increasing defect rate → quality risk |
| Trigger condition | Specific event that activates a risk response | Vendor misses 2nd milestone → invoke contingency |
| Trend indicator | Direction of risk trajectory over time | Risk probability increasing over 3 sprints |
| Threshold breach | Risk metric exceeding tolerance | Budget variance > 15% |

## 5. Risk Heat Map Evolution

Track risk movement across the probability-impact matrix over time:
- New risks entering the register
- Risks moving to higher or lower severity
- Risks being closed or transferred
- Risks materializing into issues [METRIC]

## 6. Monitoring Tools and Techniques

- Risk dashboards with real-time KRI tracking
- Risk burndown charts (analogous to sprint burndown)
- Probability-impact matrix with trend arrows
- Risk velocity tracking (speed of risk movement)
- Automated threshold alerts and escalation [PLAN]

## 7. Key Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Target |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Risk exposure trend | Total risk exposure this period vs. last | Declining |
| Response effectiveness | Risks mitigated / Risks with responses | ≥ 80% |
| Emerging risk detection rate | New risks identified per period | Stable or declining |
| Risk closure rate | Risks closed / Total active risks | ≥ 15% per quarter |
| Overdue response rate | Overdue risk actions / Total actions | < 10% |
