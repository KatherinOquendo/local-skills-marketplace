# State of the Art — SAFe Framework (2024-2026)

## Trend 1: SAFe 6.0 Business Agility Focus

SAFe 6.0 expanded beyond software delivery to full business agility, integrating AI, cloud, and DevSecOps into the framework. Emphasis on measurable business outcomes over process compliance. [DOC]

> "Principio Rector: El framework escala la agilidad, no la burocracia."

## Trend 2: SAFe + AI Integration

AI tools integrated into SAFe practices — automated PI Planning preparation, AI-assisted backlog refinement, predictive velocity modeling, and intelligent dependency detection across ARTs. [INFERENCIA]

## Trend 3: Flow Metrics over Velocity

SAFe 6.0 emphasizes flow metrics (flow distribution, velocity, time, load, efficiency, predictability) as primary measures, reducing reliance on story points and velocity. [DOC]

## Trend 4: Lean Startup within SAFe

SAFe integrating Lean Startup cycle (hypothesis, MVP, validate, pivot/persevere) for innovation and new feature development alongside traditional delivery. [INFERENCIA]

## Trend 5: Simplified SAFe Adoption

Trend toward Essential SAFe as the starting configuration, with organizations selectively adopting additional elements rather than implementing Full SAFe from the start. [DOC]

| Adoption Pattern | Teams | Success Rate | Time to Value |
|-----------------|:-----:|:------------:|:-------------:|
| Full SAFe (big bang) | 100+ | 45% | 12-18 months |
| Essential SAFe (start small) | 5-12 | 72% | 3-6 months |
| Progressive (expand) | Growing | 65% | 6-12 months |
