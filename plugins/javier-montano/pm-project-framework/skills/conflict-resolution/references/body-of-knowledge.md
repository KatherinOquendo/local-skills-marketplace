# Body of Knowledge — Conflict Resolution

## 1. Foundations

Conflict resolution in project management addresses disagreements between team members, stakeholders, or between project constraints. Managed well, conflict drives innovation; managed poorly, it destroys teams. [DOC]

### 1.1 Conflict Modes (Thomas-Kilmann)

| Mode | Assertiveness | Cooperativeness | Best When |
|------|--------------|----------------|-----------|
| Competing | High | Low | Emergency decisions, safety issues [SCHEDULE] |
| Collaborating | High | High | Complex problems needing buy-in [STAKEHOLDER] |
| Compromising | Medium | Medium | Time-constrained, equally valid positions [PLAN] |
| Avoiding | Low | Low | Trivial issues, cooling-off needed [INFERENCIA] |
| Accommodating | Low | High | Relationship preservation, minor stakes [STAKEHOLDER] |

### 1.2 Common Project Conflicts

| Source | Example | Frequency |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| Resource allocation | Two teams need same engineer | High [PLAN] |
| Technical decisions | Architecture disagreements | High [METRIC] |
| Priority conflicts | Feature A vs. Feature B | High [STAKEHOLDER] |
| Schedule pressure | Quality vs. deadline trade-offs | Medium [SCHEDULE] |
| Personality clashes | Communication style differences | Medium [STAKEHOLDER] |
| Scope disagreements | What is in/out of scope | High [PLAN] |

## 2. Resolution Techniques

| Technique | Application | Facilitation Level |
|-----------|-----------|-------------------|
| Active listening | All conflicts — establish understanding first | Individual [STAKEHOLDER] |
| Interest-based negotiation | Positional disputes | Facilitated [PLAN] |
| Mediation | Interpersonal conflicts | Third-party facilitator [STAKEHOLDER] |
| Structured debate | Technical disagreements | Time-boxed format [METRIC] |
| Escalation | Unresolvable at team level | Management chain [STAKEHOLDER] |
| Working agreement refresh | Recurring friction patterns | Team ceremony [PLAN] |

## 3. Escalation Framework

| Level | Who Resolves | Timeline | Documentation |
|-------|-------------|----------|---------------|
| L1 Team | Team members directly | Same day | None required [SCHEDULE] |
| L2 Scrum Master/PM | Facilitated resolution | 1-2 days | Brief log [DOC] |
| L3 Management | Manager-mediated | 1 week | Formal documentation [STAKEHOLDER] |
| L4 Executive | Sponsor/steering decision | 2 weeks | Decision record [STAKEHOLDER] |
