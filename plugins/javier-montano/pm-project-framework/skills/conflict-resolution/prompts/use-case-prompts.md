# Use-Case Prompts — Conflict Resolution

## Prompt 1: Conflict Assessment and Resolution Plan
**When**: Active conflict detected in team or between stakeholders.
**Context variables**: `{conflict_description}`, `{parties_involved}`, `{impact_on_project}`
**Deliver**:
> Assess `{conflict_description}` between `{parties_involved}`. Classify conflict type and recommend TKI mode. Design resolution approach with facilitation script, timeline, and success criteria. Assess `{impact_on_project}` if unresolved.

## Prompt 2: Team Working Agreement Refresh
**When**: Recurring friction patterns or team composition change.
**Context variables**: `{team_composition}`, `{friction_patterns}`, `{current_agreements}`
**Deliver**:
> Analyze `{friction_patterns}` against `{current_agreements}` for `{team_composition}`. Identify gaps in working agreements. Design facilitated workshop to create updated agreements addressing specific friction points.

## Prompt 3: Escalation Decision Framework
**When**: Conflict unresolved at team level, considering escalation.
**Context variables**: `{conflict_history}`, `{resolution_attempts}`, `{project_impact}`
**Deliver**:
> Evaluate whether to escalate `{conflict_history}` given `{resolution_attempts}`. Apply escalation criteria. If escalation warranted, prepare briefing for next-level authority with context, impact, and recommended resolution options.
