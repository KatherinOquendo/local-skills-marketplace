# State of the Art — Team Performance & Health Tracking (2024-2026)

> Current trends, emerging tools, and open debates in team performance measurement.

---

## 1. Dominant Trends

### 1.1 DORA/SPACE Metrics as Industry Standard

- **What's happening:** DORA metrics (4 key metrics) combined with SPACE framework (Satisfaction, Performance, Activity, Communication, Efficiency) provide a comprehensive, balanced view of developer team performance. Organizations adopting both frameworks report better outcomes than single-metric approaches.
- **Impact on skill:** Include both delivery metrics (DORA) and human metrics (SPACE) in every team performance assessment. Neither alone tells the full story.
- **Evidence:** Forsgren, N. et al. (2024). *SPACE Framework: A Multidimensional Approach to Developer Productivity*; DORA (2025). *State of DevOps Report*.

### 1.2 Outcome-Based Performance (Not Output-Based)

- **What's happening:** Leading organizations shift from measuring output (velocity, throughput) to measuring outcomes (customer impact, business value delivered). A team with high velocity that builds the wrong thing has high output but low performance.
- **Impact on skill:** Add outcome metrics alongside output metrics. Track feature adoption, customer satisfaction, and business impact alongside velocity and throughput.
- **Evidence:** PMI (2024). *Value Delivery in Project Management*.

### 1.3 AI-Detected Performance Signals

- **What's happening:** AI tools analyze Git commit patterns, PR review times, Jira cycle times, and communication patterns to detect team performance trends without requiring manual data collection.
- **Impact on skill:** Recommend automated data collection where available. Manual performance tracking scales poorly beyond 3 sprints.
- **Evidence:** LinearB (2025). *Engineering Metrics Platform*; Jellyfish (2025). *Engineering Management Platform*.

---

## 2. Open Debates

### 2.1 Should Velocity Be Shared With Management?
- Velocity is a planning tool, not a performance evaluation tool. Sharing velocity with management incentivizes inflation. However, management needs capacity visibility. Compromise: share throughput (items delivered) and predictability (% commitment met).

### 2.2 Team vs. Individual Performance
- Team performance metrics are standard. Individual metrics are controversial (creates competition, discourages collaboration). Emerging consensus: measure teams, develop individuals.
