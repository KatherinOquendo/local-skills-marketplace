# State of the Art — Waterfall Assessment (2024-2026)

## Trend 1: Hybrid Maturity Models

Organizations no longer assess "waterfall vs. agile" in isolation. Modern maturity frameworks evaluate the organization's ability to select and execute the right methodology for each project type. Assessment now includes "methodology selection maturity" as a dimension. [DOC]

> "Principio Rector: La madurez no es adoptar una metodología; es saber cuándo aplicar cada una."

## Trend 2: Data-Driven Assessment

Replacing subjective maturity scoring with quantitative evidence. Schedule predictability index (SPI variance across projects), cost predictability (CPI stability), and defect escape rate serve as objective maturity proxies. Organizations at Level 4+ show SPI standard deviation < 0.15 across their portfolio. [METRIC]

## Trend 3: Predictability as Primary Indicator

The shift from "did the project succeed?" to "did we predict the outcome accurately?" High-maturity organizations deliver within 5-10% of baseline estimates consistently. Assessment now measures prediction accuracy as the primary maturity indicator rather than process compliance. [INFERENCIA]

| Maturity Level | Schedule Prediction Accuracy | Cost Prediction Accuracy |
|:--------------:|:----------------------------:|:------------------------:|
| Level 1 | ±50% or unmeasured | ±50% or unmeasured |
| Level 2 | ±30% | ±35% |
| Level 3 | ±15% | ±20% |
| Level 4 | ±10% | ±10% |
| Level 5 | ±5% with confidence intervals | ±5% with Monte Carlo |

## Trend 4: Continuous Assessment

Moving from periodic (annual) maturity assessments to continuous monitoring through integrated toolchains. Project management platforms automatically track process adherence, gate compliance, and documentation completeness, providing real-time maturity dashboards. [INFERENCIA]

## Trend 5: AI-Assisted Gap Analysis

AI models analyze project artifacts (schedules, risk registers, status reports) to automatically detect maturity gaps without manual review. Pattern recognition identifies common failure modes and recommends targeted interventions based on organizational context. [SUPUESTO]
