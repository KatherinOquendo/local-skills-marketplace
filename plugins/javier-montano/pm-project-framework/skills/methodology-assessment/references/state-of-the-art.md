# Methodology Assessment — State of the Art (2024-2026)

> PMO-APEX Skill Reference | Trends & Innovation | Last updated: 2026-03-17

---

## Trend 1: Hybrid as Default
- 70%+ of organizations now use hybrid approaches rather than pure Agile or Waterfall (PMI Pulse 2025) [DOC]
- Methodology selection shifting from "which framework" to "which combination for this context" [INFERENCIA]
- Impact: Assessment models must evaluate hybrid fitness, not just single-methodology scores [PLAN]

## Trend 2: AI-Assisted Methodology Recommendation
- AI models analyzing project characteristics and historical outcomes to recommend methodology [INFERENCIA]
- Pattern matching across thousands of project profiles for evidence-based selection [INFERENCIA]
- Impact: Data-driven recommendations supplementing expert judgment [PLAN]

## Trend 3: Continuous Methodology Adaptation
- Organizations moving from one-time methodology selection to continuous methodology tuning [DOC]
- Retrospective-driven methodology adjustments within projects, not just between them [PLAN]
- Impact: Assessment becomes iterative checkpoint, not one-time decision [PLAN]

## Trend 4: Flow-Based Methodology Selection
- Flow metrics (lead time, throughput) used as objective criteria for methodology effectiveness [METRIC]
- Teams measuring actual flow regardless of methodology label to identify what works [PLAN]
- Impact: Empirical evidence replacing dogmatic methodology preferences [PLAN]

## Trend 5: Team-Centric Selection
- Methodology selection increasingly considers team preferences and psychological safety [STAKEHOLDER]
- Teams with methodology autonomy showing higher engagement and delivery predictability [INFERENCIA]
- Impact: Assessment includes team voice, not just project characteristics [STAKEHOLDER]
