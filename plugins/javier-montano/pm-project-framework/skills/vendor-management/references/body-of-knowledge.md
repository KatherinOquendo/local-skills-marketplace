# Body of Knowledge — Vendor Management

## 1. Definition and Scope

Vendor management encompasses the processes, practices, and governance structures for selecting, onboarding, monitoring, and managing external suppliers throughout the project lifecycle. It ensures vendors deliver contracted value while maintaining quality, compliance, and relationship health. [DOC]

## 3. Vendor Management Lifecycle

| Phase | Activities | Key Output |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| Selection | RFP, evaluation, scoring, negotiation | Vendor shortlist, contract [PLAN] |
| Onboarding | Access setup, KT, tooling, integration | Onboarding checklist |
| Delivery | Performance monitoring, SLA tracking, communication | Performance reports [METRIC] |
| Governance | Reviews, escalations, change management | Governance minutes |
| Offboarding | KT, access revocation, retrospective | Transition report |

## 4. SLA Framework

| SLA Category | Example Metrics | Measurement |
|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| Delivery | On-time delivery rate ≥ 90% | Sprint/milestone tracking [METRIC] |
| Quality | Defect density < 2/KLOC | QA metrics |
| Responsiveness | Issue response < 4 hours | Ticket system |
| Communication | Status report timeliness | Calendar adherence |
| Availability | Key personnel availability ≥ 95% | Attendance tracking |

## 5. Vendor Performance Scorecard

| Dimension | Weight | Green | Amber | Red |
|-----------|:------:|:-----:|:-----:|:---:|
| Delivery quality | 30% | ≥ 4.0/5.0 | 3.0-3.9 | < 3.0 |
| Timeline adherence | 25% | ≥ 90% on-time | 75-89% | < 75% |
| Communication | 20% | Proactive | Reactive | Unresponsive |
| Value delivery | 15% | Exceeds expectations | Meets | Below |
| Relationship health | 10% | Collaborative | Transactional | Adversarial |
