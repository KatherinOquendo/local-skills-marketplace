# State of the Art — Quality Audit (2024-2026)

## Trend 1: Continuous Auditing with Automated Evidence Collection

Traditional periodic audits are evolving into continuous auditing models where automated tools monitor compliance in real-time, flagging deviations as they occur. Evidence collection is automated from CI/CD pipelines, test suites, and process management tools. [DOC]

> "Principio Rector: La calidad no se inspecciona al final, se construye en cada paso."

## Trend 2: AI-Assisted Audit Analysis

LLM-based tools analyze documentation, code, and process artifacts to identify potential non-conformances before human auditors review. AI reduces audit preparation time by 50% and catches subtle pattern-based issues. [INFERENCIA]

| Audit Phase | AI Contribution | Time Savings |
|-------------|----------------|-------------|
| Planning | Risk-based scope recommendation | 30% |
| Preparation | Automated checklist generation | 40% |
| Execution | Pattern detection in evidence | 50% |
| Analysis | Non-conformance classification | 35% |
| Reporting | Draft report generation | 60% |

## Trend 3: Risk-Based Audit Prioritization

Audit resources focused on highest-risk areas using data-driven risk models. Low-risk processes audited less frequently while high-risk areas receive continuous monitoring. [DOC]

## Trend 4: Agile-Compatible Quality Auditing

Quality audits adapted for agile environments with sprint-level micro-audits replacing annual comprehensive audits. Definition of Done serves as the primary audit criteria within agile contexts. [INFERENCIA]

## Trend 5: Audit as Enablement

Shift from compliance-focused auditing to enablement-focused consulting. Auditors act as quality coaches, helping teams improve processes rather than merely identifying failures. [DOC]

| Audit Approach | Focus | Relationship | Outcome |
|---------------|-------|-------------|---------|
| Traditional | Compliance | Adversarial | NCR report |
| Modern | Improvement | Collaborative | Improvement roadmap |
| Coaching | Enablement | Partnership | Process maturity growth |
