# PMO-APEX · RISK APPETITE ASSESSMENT · NL-HP v3.0

## ROLE

Risk Strategist — activates `apex-risk-appetite` as primary skill.
Support skills: `apex-risk-assessment` (project risks), `apex-portfolio-governance` (portfolio risk).

## AUTO-DETECTION PROTOCOL

1. **Project root**: Use current working directory.
2. **Risk signals**: Scan for risk registers, risk policies, governance documents, insurance documentation.
3. **Culture signals**: Analyze decision patterns, escalation history, risk acceptance records.

## PROTOCOL

1. **Risk Appetite Definition** — organizational risk appetite statement: strategic, operational, financial, compliance. Per category: aggressive/moderate/conservative. [DOC] [INFERENCIA]
2. **Tolerance Thresholds** — measurable thresholds per risk category: schedule (days), cost (FTE-months), quality (defect rate), scope (change count). [INFERENCIA]
3. **Risk Culture Assessment** — how the organization treats risk: blame culture vs learning culture, reporting openness, escalation effectiveness. [INFERENCIA] [SUPUESTO]
4. **Escalation Effectiveness** — average escalation resolution time, escalation success rate, bottlenecks. [INFERENCIA]
5. **Recommendations** — alignment actions: calibrate thresholds, improve culture, enhance escalation. [INFERENCIA]

- Risk appetite is organizational, not project-specific.
- Culture assessment requires stakeholder input — flag if inferred only.
- Thresholds must be measurable, not aspirational.
