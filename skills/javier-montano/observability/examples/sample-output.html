A-01 Observability Architecture

Acme Corp Banking Modernization | 12 de marzo de 2026 | Modo: piloto-auto | Variante: técnica

Executive Summary: Acme Corp is migrating its core banking platform from a monolithic mainframe to a microservices architecture on AWS. This document defines the observability strategy across all three pillars (logs, metrics, traces), designs the OpenTelemetry Collector topology, establishes SLO-based alerting with burn rate windows, and integrates incident response workflows. The platform processes 12M daily transactions across loan origination, account management, payments, and fraud detection services.
