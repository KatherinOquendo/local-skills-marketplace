A-01 Performance Engineering

Acme Corp Banking Modernization | 12 de marzo de 2026 | Modo: piloto-auto | Variante: técnica

Executive Summary: Acme Corp's core banking platform processes 12M transactions daily. Current p99 latency for loan origination (8.1s) exceeds the 5s target, requiring immediate optimization of the credit bureau integration. This assessment establishes performance baselines, load testing strategies for banking peak periods, USL-based capacity models, and SLO targets aligned with OCC regulatory requirements.
