A-01 Security Architecture

Acme Corp Banking Modernization | 12 de marzo de 2026 | Modo: piloto-auto | Variante: técnica

Executive Summary: Acme Corp's core banking platform processes 12M daily transactions across loan origination, payments, and account management. This security architecture establishes STRIDE threat modeling for all critical flows, a 36-month Zero Trust adoption roadmap (Crawl/Walk/Run), RBAC+ABAC hybrid authorization with 7 role definitions, AES-256 encryption at rest and TLS 1.3 in transit, a 6-stage security pipeline integrated into CI/CD, and compliance mapping for PCI-DSS 4.0, SOC 2 Type II, and OCC regulatory requirements.
