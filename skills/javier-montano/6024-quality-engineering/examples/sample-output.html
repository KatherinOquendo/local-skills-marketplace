A-01 Quality Engineering

Acme Corp Banking Modernization | 12 de marzo de 2026 | Modo: piloto-auto | Variante: técnica

Executive Summary: Acme Corp is migrating its core banking platform from a COBOL mainframe to Java/Spring Boot microservices. This quality engineering framework establishes maturity assessment (current Level 2, target Level 4 in 12 months), test strategy aligned with the microservices diamond shape, automation architecture using JUnit 5 + Testcontainers + Pact, CI/CD quality gates at 5 pipeline stages, and quality metrics with DORA benchmarking. Critical gap: zero contract testing between 14 services and no automated regression suite for the loan origination workflow.
