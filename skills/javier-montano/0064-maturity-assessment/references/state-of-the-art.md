# Maturity Assessment — State of the Art (2024-2026)

> PMO-APEX Skill Reference | Trends & Innovation | Last updated: 2026-03-17

---

## Trend 1: Continuous Maturity Monitoring
- Shift from annual assessments to continuous maturity tracking via automated process compliance monitoring [DOC]
- Tools like Planview, Clarity, and ServiceNow embedding maturity indicators into operational dashboards [DOC]
- Impact: Real-time maturity visibility replaces point-in-time assessments [PLAN]

## Trend 2: AI-Driven Maturity Diagnostics
- ML models analyzing project artifacts, communication patterns, and delivery data to infer maturity levels [INFERENCIA]
- Pattern recognition across hundreds of projects identifies organizational capability gaps automatically [INFERENCIA]
- Impact: Objective, data-driven maturity scoring at lower cost; requires data quality [PLAN]

## Trend 3: Agile-Native Maturity Models
- Traditional maturity models adding agile-specific dimensions (flow, continuous delivery, team autonomy) [DOC]
- New frameworks like Business Agility Institute assessment designed for hybrid organizations (2024-2026) [DOC]
- Impact: Maturity models now evaluate both predictive and adaptive capabilities [PLAN]

## Trend 4: Value-Outcome Maturity
- Assessments shifting from process compliance to value delivery outcomes [DOC]
- Metrics like time-to-value, customer satisfaction, and benefits realization supplementing process scores [METRIC]
- Impact: High process maturity with low outcomes gets appropriate low rating [PLAN]

## Trend 5: Cross-Industry Benchmarking Platforms
- Cloud-based platforms enabling anonymous cross-industry maturity benchmarking [DOC]
- Organizations compare maturity profiles against sector peers with statistical significance [METRIC]
- Impact: Executive-friendly benchmarking data supporting investment decisions [STAKEHOLDER]
