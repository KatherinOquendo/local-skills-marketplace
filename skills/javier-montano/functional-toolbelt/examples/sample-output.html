Acceptance Criteria

Story: As a banking customer, I want to transfer funds between accounts so that I can manage my finances in real-time.

Scenario 1: Successful Internal Transfer (Happy Path)

Given source account ACCT-001 has balance $12,500.00 And destination ACCT-002 is an internal Acme account When customer initiates transfer of $3,000.00 Then system debits $3,000 from ACCT-001 within 2 seconds And credits $3,000 to ACCT-002 And receipt TXN-2026-XXXXX is generated

Scenario 2: Insufficient Funds (Negative)

Given account ACCT-001 has balance $500.00 When customer initiates transfer of $750.00 Then system displays "Insufficient funds. Available: $500.00" And no debit is performed

Scenario 3: Daily Limit Boundary

Given Standard tier, daily limit $5,000, already transferred $4,800 When customer transfers $200.00 Then transfer approved (cumulative = $5,000) When customer attempts another $1.00 Then blocked: "Daily limit exceeded. Resets 00:00 ET."
Anti-Pattern Self-Check All scenarios verified: no vague Then clauses, specific dollar values in Given, no implementation details, negative + boundary + resilience cases included.
