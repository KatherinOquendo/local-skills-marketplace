# Phase 1 Completion Checklist

**Purpose**: Verify the product brief is ready for handoff to Phase 2 (Planning).
**Gate owner**: Analyst agent or human product owner.
**Rule**: Every unchecked item requires a written justification or the brief is not ready.

---

## Product Brief Readiness

- [ ] **Problem statement exists** with at least one evidence tag (`[RESEARCH]`, `[DATA]`, `[INTERVIEW]`, `[INFERENCE]`)
- [ ] **Target audience defined** with behavioral detail (not just demographics — goals, frustrations, context of use)
- [ ] **Confidence levels explicit** on all major claims (High / Medium / Low with rationale)
- [ ] **Success metrics defined** (minimum 2) with measurable targets or acknowledged unknown baselines
- [ ] **Competitive analysis completed** or a justified skip documented ("no direct competitors — internal tool" is valid; silence is not)
- [ ] **Alternatives explored** — at least one alternative approach documented with reason for rejection
- [ ] **Scope boundaries set** — minimum 2 explicit out-of-scope items listed
- [ ] **Stakeholder alignment documented** — key stakeholders identified and their input reflected or disagreements recorded
- [ ] **All required product brief sections present** per `references/schemas.md` (title in frontmatter, problem, audience, metrics, scope)
- [ ] **Brief committed to Git** at `planning_artifacts/product-brief.md` with valid YAML frontmatter
- [ ] **Phase 1 time-box respected** — analysis completed within the time-box for the project size (see `references/phase-1-analysis.md` [R1])
- [ ] **No unresolved open questions** that would block Phase 2 — remaining questions are documented with owners and target dates

---

## Handoff Verification

- [ ] Analyst has reviewed the brief for internal consistency (claims do not contradict each other)
- [ ] Brief is self-contained — a PM agent can write a PRD from it without requesting additional context
