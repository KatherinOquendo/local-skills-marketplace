## 1. Onboarding Frameworks

### Bauer's Four C's Model
- **Compliance**: Legal, policy, and administrative requirements [DOC]
- **Clarification**: Role expectations, goals, and success criteria [DOC]
- **Culture**: Organizational norms, values, and team dynamics [STAKEHOLDER]
- **Connection**: Relationships with colleagues, mentors, and stakeholders [STAKEHOLDER]

### 30-60-90 Day Model
| Period | Focus | Milestone |
|--------|-------|-----------|
| Day 1-30 | Learn | Environment setup, context understanding, first contribution [SCHEDULE] |
| Day 31-60 | Contribute | Independent task completion, ceremony participation [SCHEDULE] |
| Day 61-90 | Own | Independent delivery, knowledge sharing with others [SCHEDULE] |

## 2. Knowledge Transfer Methods

| Method | Best For | Effort | Retention |
|--------|---------|:---:|:---:|
| Pair programming | Technical skills, tacit knowledge | High | Very High [PLAN] |
| Documentation | Reference knowledge, procedures | Medium | Medium [PLAN] |
| Shadowing | Process understanding, cultural norms | Medium | High [PLAN] |
| Workshops | Conceptual knowledge, methodology | Medium | Medium [PLAN] |
| Video recordings | Repeatable processes, demos | Low | Medium [PLAN] |
| Mentoring | Career and role guidance | Low-Medium | High [STAKEHOLDER] |

## 4. Buddy System Design

| Element | Guideline |
|---------|-----------|
| Pairing ratio | 1:1 (1 buddy per new member) [PLAN] |
| Buddy selection | Experienced team member, NOT the manager [STAKEHOLDER] |
| Check-in frequency | Daily (15 min) for first 2 weeks, then weekly [SCHEDULE] |
| Duration | Formal buddy period: 30 days [SCHEDULE] |
| Buddy training | 30-min orientation on buddy responsibilities [PLAN] |
