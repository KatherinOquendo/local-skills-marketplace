## Achievement Formula

### The STAR-Impact Pattern

```
[Power Verb] + [What You Did] + [Scale/Scope] + [Measurable Result]
```

### Transformation Examples

**BEFORE → AFTER** (English):

```
❌ "Managed social media accounts"
✅ "Grew Instagram following from 5K to 85K (+1,600%) in 18 months,
    generating $240K in attributed revenue through organic content strategy"

❌ "Responsible for customer service"
✅ "Redesigned customer support workflow for 50-person team, reducing
    average resolution time from 48hrs to 6hrs while improving CSAT from 72% to 94%"

❌ "Worked on the company website"
✅ "Architected and launched e-commerce platform serving 200K+ monthly users,
    achieving 99.9% uptime and 40% faster page loads through CDN optimization"

❌ "Helped with hiring"
✅ "Recruited and onboarded 35 engineers across 3 continents in 6 months,
    reducing time-to-hire by 45% through structured interview framework"
```

**ANTES → DESPUÉS** (Español):

```
❌ "Encargado de las redes sociales"
✅ "Escalé la presencia en Instagram de 5K a 85K seguidores (+1,600%) en 18 meses,
    generando $240K en ingresos atribuidos mediante estrategia de contenido orgánico"

❌ "Responsable de atención al cliente"
✅ "Rediseñé el flujo de soporte para un equipo de 50 personas, reduciendo el
    tiempo de resolución de 48hrs a 6hrs y mejorando CSAT de 72% a 94%"
```

## Quantification Cheat Sheet

When the candidate says "I don't have metrics," help them find hidden numbers:

| What They Say | Ask This | Metric Format |
|---------------|----------|---------------|
| "I managed a team" | How many people? | "Led X-person team" |
| "I saved money" | How much? Over what period? | "Reduced costs by $XK/year" |
| "I improved the process" | By how much faster/better? | "Improved X by Y%" |
| "I worked with clients" | How many? Total contract value? | "Managed $XM portfolio across Y clients" |
| "I built a feature" | How many users? What impact? | "Launched feature adopted by X users" |
| "I trained people" | How many? What was the result? | "Trained X employees, achieving Y% certification rate" |
| "I handled support tickets" | Volume? Resolution rate? | "Resolved X+ tickets/month at Y% satisfaction" |

## ATS Keyword Strategy

### Keyword Placement Priority
1. **Professional Summary** — 3-5 key terms
2. **Job Titles** — Match target role title exactly
3. **Bullet Points** — Weave keywords into achievements naturally
4. **Skills Section** — Comprehensive keyword list
5. **Certifications** — Full names + acronyms

### Keyword Density Rules
- Include both the acronym AND the full term: "SEO (Search Engine Optimization)"
- Mirror the exact phrases from target job descriptions
- Don't keyword-stuff — each mention should read naturally
- Aim for top 10-15 keywords appearing 2-3 times each across the document

## Cover Letter Framework

### Structure (English)
```
[Opening Hook — 1-2 sentences that grab attention]
↓
[Connection — Why THIS company and THIS role]
↓
[Achievement 1 — Most relevant, with metrics]
↓
[Achievement 2 — Demonstrates cultural fit]
↓
[Forward Look — What you'll bring to the role]
↓
[Close — Confident, clear call to action]
```

### Structure (Spanish)
```
[Apertura — 1-2 oraciones que capten atención]
↓
[Conexión — Por qué ESTA empresa y ESTE rol]
↓
[Logro 1 — El más relevante, con métricas]
↓
[Logro 2 — Demuestra fit cultural]
↓
[Visión — Lo que aportarás al rol]
↓
[Cierre — Seguro, con llamado a acción claro]
```

### Opening Lines That Work
**Instead of**: "I am writing to express my interest in the [Position] role at [Company]"

**Try**:
- "When I saw [Company] was looking for someone to [specific challenge], I knew my experience [doing X] would be a perfect fit."
- "After spending [X years] building [specific thing], I'm excited about the opportunity to bring that expertise to [Company]'s [specific initiative]."
- "Tres años liderando la transformación digital en [Empresa] me prepararon exactamente para el desafío que [Empresa Target] enfrenta hoy."

## LinkedIn Optimization

### Headline Formula (120 chars max)
```
[Title] | [Value Prop] | [Key Skill 1] • [Key Skill 2] • [Key Skill 3]
```
Example: "Senior Product Manager | Building AI products that 10x user engagement | Strategy • Analytics • Growth"

### About Section Structure
```
Line 1-3 (visible above fold — CRITICAL):
[Hook + current role + biggest achievement with number]

Lines 4-8:
[Career narrative — how you got here and what drives you]

Lines 9-12:
[Key expertise areas with specifics]

Lines 13-15:
[What you're looking for / open to + call to action]

Final line:
[Keywords not yet mentioned, naturally integrated]
```
